Concurrency

Playlist and library runs resolve multiple tracks in parallel instead of searching one song at a time. That keeps larger music selections practical without turning the flow into a slow serial process.

Caching

Accepted YouTube matches are cached against track metadata and the matcher version. Repeated runs can reuse known matches, while matcher changes can safely invalidate older decisions.

Match scoring

The resolver checks title coverage, artist signals, duration mismatch, source quality, and unwanted versions like covers, remixes, karaoke, slowed tracks, or live recordings before accepting a candidate.
