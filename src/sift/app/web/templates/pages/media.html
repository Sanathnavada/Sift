Media Workflow

Turn reels, posts, carousels, and videos into searchable text.

Mixed media

A post may contain image text, voiceover, captions, slides, or video. The backend normalizes the provided Posts , detects the media shape, and chooses the right extraction path.

Processing stack

Images are processed with Microsoft Florence-2. Videos and reels are transcribed with OpenAI Whisper. The extracted text is then combined into readable outputs.

Long-running jobs

Media extraction can take time, especially for reels, carousels, profiles, and saved collections. Sift runs these as background tasks with visible progress instead of blocking the page while the work completes.
